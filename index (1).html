Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Absolutely. All new members get a 14-day free trial with full access to Premium features. No credit card required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Annual subscriptions can also be paid via bank transfer.

Can I share my account?

Each plan includes multiple concurrent streams for household use. Director's Cut members can create separate profiles for each family member.
